Hotel Recommender

In this project, the objective is to transform implicit information provided by users into explicit features for a
content-based hotel recommender engine.

We conceptualised this engine to simplify hotel search process for travelers who have a particular preference
in mind, and are not keen to trawl through numerous options that may not fit their requirements.

To use the engine, a traveler simply selects a well-known hotel that he or she likes against the preferred
destination in pre-determined drop downs. He or she is then able to view ranked results according to two data
attributes: via common features or via reviews.

This recommender is built on content-based filtering methodology by collating item-item similarity matrices.
This website is built on top of Flask.

For the purpose of this project. I used dataset of "515K Hotel Revies Data in Europe" available on Kaggle.
